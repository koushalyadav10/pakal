River Flood Awareness

What is a River Flood?

A river flood occurs when water levels in a river rise and overflow onto the surrounding land. River floods can be caused by heavy rainfall, snowmelt, or a combination of factors.

How to Stay Safe

  • Monitor river levels: Keep track of weather forecasts and flood warnings.
  • Have an emergency plan: Know evacuation routes and have a family communication plan.
  • Prepare an emergency kit: Include essentials like water, food, medications, and a first aid kit.
  • Keep important documents in a waterproof container.
  • Do not walk or drive through flooded areas.

What to Do During a River Flood

If you are in an area affected by a river flood:

  1. Follow evacuation orders from local authorities.
  2. Move to higher ground and avoid low-lying areas.
  3. Avoid contact with floodwaters, which may be contaminated.
  4. Stay informed through local news and weather updates.
  5. Check on neighbors, especially those who may need assistance.
